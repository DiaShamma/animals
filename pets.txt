3 Animals that could be a pet
1-Dogs: Dogs are one of the most popular pets around the world. They are known for their loyalty, affection, and intelligence. They come in a variety of breeds and sizes, so you can choose one that suits your lifestyle and preferences.
2-Cats: Cats are also a popular choice for pets. They are independent and require less attention than dogs, making them a good choice for people who have busy lifestyles. They are also known for their playful and curious nature, and can be very affectionate with their owners.
3-Guinea pigs: Guinea pigs are a small, gentle species of rodent that make great pets for children and adults alike. They are social animals and enjoy living in pairs or small groups. They are also easy to care for and require a relatively small amount of space
